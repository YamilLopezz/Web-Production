What is the UEFA Champions League?



The UEFA Champions League is played yearly by Europes Top Clubs. The Top 4 clubs from each League or Region are giving the opportunity to win the most wanted trophy by a club. Introduced in 1955 and known as the Europeans cup the tournement started as a knockout tournamnet by the Champions of each domestic league. As time passed the name was changed to the UEFA CHhampions League and allowed for the top 4 teams in each league to participate.
